Pokemon Trading Card Game

Basics: In the Pokemon TCG, your Pokemon battle your opponent's Pokemon. The first player to take all of their prize cards win. Also, if your opponent has no Pokemon left in play, or no cards left to draw at the beginning of their turn, you win the game! Click Here For an In depth explanation.
