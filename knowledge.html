Terminology

  1. Pokemon = A creature that can use special moves that identify with their type
  2. Pokedex = A device used to document all the pokemon you meet
  3. Evolutions = When a pokemon meets certian criteria
  4. Types=All pokemon have a type. For example the starters are water type, fire type, and grass type
