Generation 3:The Hoenn Region

Generation 3 came with 3 games, Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald. Also there were 135 new pokemon added, the total pokemon number at 386. The starters are Treecko, Torchic, and Mudkip. The legendary pokemon are Kyorgre, targeted by evil Team Aqua, and Groudon, targeted by Team Magma. In Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald the concept of double battles was first intoduced. Generation 3 added the most abilities out of the generation, 77. Generation 3 was the first to intoduce multiple Legendary Trio, multiple mythical pokemon, NOT introduce any eevee-lutions, and have multiple paired versions of the game. It also added two pseudo-legendary pokemon, Salamance and Metagross.
